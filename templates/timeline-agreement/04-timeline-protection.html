Timeline Protection

Timeline Protection Strategies

Built-in Buffer Time

We've included reasonable buffer time in each phase to accommodate:

  • Normal revision cycles
  • Minor scope clarifications
  • Unexpected technical challenges
  • Holiday and vacation schedules

Revision Impact on Timeline

Normal revisions within scope are already factored into this timeline.

Minor changes: May add 1-3 days to current phase
Major changes: May require timeline renegotiation
Scope additions: Will require formal timeline adjustment

Change Request Process:

  1. 1. Submit change request in writing
  2. 2. We'll assess timeline impact
  3. 3. Provide updated timeline estimate
  4. 4. Proceed only after your approval

Delay Management

If We Need to Adjust Timeline:

  • • Immediate notification with explanation
  • • Revised timeline with new completion date
  • • Options to expedite if needed
  • • Clear communication about impacts

If You Need More Time:

  • • Let us know as soon as possible
  • • We'll adjust the timeline accordingly
  • • No penalties for reasonable delays
  • • Clear communication about new schedule

We'll Alert You Immediately If:

  • Any delays are anticipated
  • Additional time is needed for quality
  • External factors affect the schedule
  • Scope changes impact the timeline
